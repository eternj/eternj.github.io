How to Sideload Kodi to Apple TV Using atvloadly

Posted on 2026-02-15

Overview
The goal of this article is to share how I installed (sideloaded) Kodi to my Apple TV 4K (2nd Gen). Kodi is a media center and entertainment hub that I have been using since pre-Kodi era (when Kodi was XBMC). It has a beautiful interface and is very easy to use. I mainly use Kodi to watch and record real-time TV programs, as well as watch ripped movies. Kodi has powerful add-ons that can expand and enhance its features and capabilities. Kodi officially supports Windows, linux, and android. Unfortunately, Apple TV, which runs tvOS, does not have an official version of Kodi in App Store. This means we cannot download and install Kodi from App Store. Instead, we’ll need to find some unofficial way to install or sideload it to Apple TVs.

atvloadly is a web service that supports sideloading apps on Apple TV. In this article, I’ll share how I setup an atvloadly docker and how I used atvloadly to sideload Kodi to my Apple TV. Hopefully this will help someone else who’s looking to do the same.

Workflow and Prerequisites

  1. Workflow

    • Setup an atvloadly docker.
    • Add a free Apple account to atvloadly.
    • Pair the atvloadly web service with the Apple TV.
    • Sideload Kodi to the Apple TV.

  2. Prerequisites

    • A computer running linux. Unfortunately, this method does NOT work on Windows. It doesn’t need to be a full-fledged laptop or desktop. You can use a Raspberry Pi if you have one lying around. I used a single board computer called Rock64 running a stripped down version of Debian.
    • A burned Apple account. As noted on the atvloadly website, it is a good ideal to use a dedicated Apple ID or Apple account for sideloading. A free Apple account will work. I just created a new free Apple account for this purpose. You can create one through Apple Developer Account.
    • A phone for two-factor authorization (2FA) verification. When creating a new Apple account for sideloading, you need a phone for 2FA. atvloadly needs to be authorized as a trusted device (it will be virtualized as a MacBook). When adding the Apple account to atvloadly, Apple will send a 2FA verification code to the registered phone number and you will need to enter the code and authorize it.

Add Apple Account to atvloadly
First thing to do is to add your newly-created Apple account to atvloadly following the steps below:

  1. On the atvloadly homepage, select “PREFERENCES –> Accounts”.
  2. Click “ADD ACCOUNT” button.
  3. The “Login Apple Account” window opens, like this:
    ![[Pasted image 20260214205157.png]]
  4. Input the email and password of your newly-created Apple account and click “LOGIN” button.
  5. A second window will pop up and ask you to input a security code that was just sent to your phone. Enter that code and click “OK”.
  6. After that you should see that your new Apple account is listed on the Accounts page.

Pair Apple TV
Now it’s time to pair your Apple TV in atvloadly. My Apple TV 4K (2nd Gen) does not have a usb port so I couldn’t use a USB cable to connect it to my PC. I had to pair it wirelessly. To do this, I followed the steps below:

  1. On your Apple TV, open the Settings menu. Then select “Remote and Devices –> Remote App and Devices”. This will cause your Apple TV to enter the pairing mode.
  2. Refresh the atvloadly homepage. Now you should be able to see your Apple TV with word “Pairable”.
  3. Click on your Apple TV to pair. Your Apple TV will display a pairing code on your TV. Enter that code in your atvloadly page. Now your Apple TV should be successfully paired in atvloadly.

Sideload Kodi to Your Apple TV
Once you successfully paired your Apple TV, you can start sideloading to your Apple TV using atvloadly. Follow the steps below:

  1. Download the unsigned Kodi ipa file from here. Make sure you download the one for tvOS, not iOS.
  2. Open atvloadly home page on your computer. Click the Apple TV that you paired in the last step. A new window will pop up, like this:
    ![[Pasted image 20260214210435.png]]
  3. Click “CHOOSE FILE” button and select kodi-tvos_21.2.ipa you just downloaded.
  4. Select your Apple account from the dropdown list.
  5. Click “INSTALL” button and atvloadly will do its magic and install Kodi to your Apple TV. This step will take a while since Kodi is a decent size app so be paitent. Behind the scene, I believe atvloadly does the following: 1) creating a developer certificate from your Apple account, 2) signing the Kodi ipa using that certificate, and 3) installing Kodi to your Apple TV after signing.
  6. After atvloadly shows “Done” in its message box, Kodi has been successfully installed to your Apple TV. You can now go to your Apple TV and enjoy Kodi -:)

Some Notes

  • As of this writing, the latest atvloadly version is v0.3.3. The latest tvOS is v26.1. They work together pretty well.
  • Since I used a free Apple account to sideload Kodi to my Apple TV. The certificate is only good for 7 days which means Kodi will stop working after 7 days. However, by default, atvloadly will automatically refresh (re-sign) the Kodi app on Day 6. That way, you can keep using Kodi without manually sideloading Kodi again after 7 days.
  • You can use a paid Apple developer account to sideload Kodi. In that case the installation is good for 365 days. However, since atvloadly automatically refresh the installation every 7 days, you don’t need to pay for a real Apple developer account to have a “permanent” Kodi app on your Apple TV.
  • For Kodi, I only use two add-ons with it: NextPVR and Jellyfin add-ons. NextPVR add-on allows me to watch over-the-air (OTA) TV programs. Jellyfin add-on allows me to watch ripped TV series and blueray movies. Both add-ons work flawlessly for me. I haven’t tried other add-ons in Kodi.

创建自己的独立博客（一）

Posted on 2020-03-27 Edited on 2026-02-15

我喜欢折腾些乱七八糟的东西，从木匠活儿到组乐队到装电脑到折腾家用电器，可谓五花八门保罗万象。每次自己花时间花精力琢磨后完成一件事还是很有成就感的。既然花了时间花了精力，最好想办法记录下来，这样以后再折腾同样的东西或者要维修升级的时候就不用再重复折腾一遍。“好记性不如烂笔头”，脑袋再好使也不如笔下记的东西可靠，是不？而且记录的过程也是一个再思考的过程，能让我更好的理清事情的来龙去脉，加深自己的理解。

用博客记录自己折腾的项目是个很好的方法。那么怎么才能拥有自己的博客呢？Hexo是一个理想的解决方案。

什么是Hexo

Hexo是一个快速简洁高效的博客框架工具。它可以把Markdown文档快速解析成静态HTML页面，并支持各种漂亮的主题。本文首先介绍如何用Hexo在本地计算机上建立一个自己的博客。后续文章会介绍如何把本地计算机上的博客文章发布到GitHub上分享给互联网上的朋友。
